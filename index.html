Nutritionist Guidelines

💧 Hydration
Drink minimum 3–3.5L of water per day. As a mechanic working physically, dehydration will kill performance and slow fat loss. Start every morning with a large glass before meal 1.
🍳 Meal Prep (Dimanche)
Cook chicken, rice and potatoes in bulk every Sunday. Pre-portion into containers. This makes hitting macros on busy work days non-negotiable.
⚙️ Carb Timing
Put the majority of carbs around training (Meal 3 lunch & Meal 4 pre-workout). On rest days, reduce Meal 4 carbs by 50% — swap sweet potato for extra veggies or a smaller portion.
🥩 Beef Rotation
Beef is capped at 200g/day (fat content). On days without beef, replace with: 200g chicken for dinner, or 150g canned sardines for higher omega-3 intake. Rotate sardines 2–3x per week.
🚫 What to Avoid
Merguez, fried food (méchoui style), Algerian pastries (baklawa, zlabia), soft drinks, harissa in excess, too much bread. These will break the deficit silently without feeling like a cheat.
🌿 Free Vegetables
The following can be eaten freely (no tracking needed): lettuce, cucumber, tomato, courgette, green pepper, spinach, fresh herbs (coriander, parsley), lemon juice. These add volume, fiber and micronutrients with near-zero calories.
